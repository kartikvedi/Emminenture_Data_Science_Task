Your questions about commercial property answered

What is commercial property?
Commercial property, also known as commercial real estate, is property or land used for business purposes rather than as a living space.  It includes offices, industrial units, leisure and retail – both to let and for sale.
How is a commercial property valued?

The amount a commercial property is worth is subject to a range of factors, which include:

  • The price of recently sold properties in the surrounding areas
  • Market trends
  • Availability of similar properties
  • The condition of your property
  • The level of income the property generates
Who do I speak to when preparing to buy or rent a commercial property?

It is highly recommended that you choose a commercial agent who is a member of an accredited trade body, such as Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS).

We recommend you choose an agent who is an expert in your chosen sector or type of property.

How do I market a commercial property?

You can market the property with an agent of your choice, but it is important to make sure that your agent understands exactly what you are looking to achieve.

It can be useful to provide your agent with the initial relevant information to assist in marketing your property. Your agent will normally add to this with detailed descriptions and photographs, using their in-depth knowledge and history of previous sales.

We would also recommend looking to see how the agent advertises their existing commercial properties on Rightmove.
